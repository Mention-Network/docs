**Cookies and Automatically Collected Data**

As you navigate through and interact with the website and the Services, you may be asked to consent to use cookies, which are small files placed on the hard drive of your computer or mobile device, and web beacons, which are small electronic files located on pages of the website, to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns.

The data that is automatically collected from cookies and web beacons may include information from your web browser (such as browser type and browser language) and details of your visits to the website, including traffic data, location data and logs, page views, length of visit and website navigation paths as well as information about your device and internet connection, including your IP address and how you interact with the Services.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area and you are under the age of 16, please do not submit any personal data through the Services and the website. Personal Information is not collected or processed for a child, where a child under the GDPR is defined as an individual below the age of 16 years.

**Your Rights With Regard to the Personal Data Processing**

In connection with the accessing and browsing of the website and using the Services, you shall be entitled to exercise certain rights laid down by the GDPR and outlined herein below; however, the exercise of some of those rights may not be possible in relation to the website and Services taking into account of the Services’ nature, manner, form and other applicable circumstances.

- **Right to Access**: you may request all personal data via email.
- **Right to Rectification**: The exercise of this given right directly depends on the data category concerned: if it concerns online identifiers, then their rectification isn’t possible, but such categories of personal data as an email address may be rectified by sending a request via email.
- **Right to Erasure** (Right to be Forgotten): the personal data may be deleted by a request via email.
- **Consent Withdrawal Right**: You shall be entitled to withdraw consent to the processing of  personal data to which you provided your consent. In particular, you can change your cookie choices by using a cookie consent tool built into the website. You can exercise your right to withdraw consent by unsubscribing from an email newsletter.
- **Automated Decision-Making, Profiling**: Neither is being carried out; however,  your consent will be sought before carrying out any such activities.

You shall have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.

**Personal Data Storage Period or Criteria for Such Storage**

Your Personal data will be stored until:

- They are necessary to render you the Services;
- Your consent is no longer valid;
- Your personal data have been deleted following your data deletion request;
- Or in other circumstances prescribed by applicable laws.

In any event, your personal data will not be stored for a period longer than necessary for the purposes of processing.
