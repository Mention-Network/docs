**Turn your network into rewards.** Our multi-level referral system lets you earn **Shards**, the core points of Mention Network, every time someone you invite joins and engages.\
And the best part? You’ll also earn from the people _they_ invite.

![Tg Image 3015248424 Pn](/images/tg_image_3015248424.png)

## 🧬 How It Works

You’ll earn **Shards** when users join through your unique link and participate in the Mention ecosystem. Our system tracks **three levels** of referrals, so your rewards stack over time.

| Referral Level | Who It Is                   | You Earn                |
| :------------- | :-------------------------- | :---------------------- |
| **Level 1**    | Direct invite (your friend) | **10%** of their Shards |
| **Level 2**    | Your friend’s invite        | **3%** of their Shards  |
| **Level 3**    | Friend of a friend’s invite | **1%** of their Shards  |

### 🚀 3 Simple Steps to Earn Shards

**1. Copy Your Referral Link**\
Head to your dashboard and grab your unique link.

**2. Share It with Friends & Channels**\
Post it wherever your network lives.

**3. Enjoy the Shards 🎉**\
Earn Shards every time someone joins. The more they engage, the more you earn\!

## 💡 Example

If your direct invite earns **1,000 Shards**,

- You get **100 Shards (10%)**
- If their invite earns 1,000 → you get **30 Shards (3%)**
- And if _their_ invite earns 1,000 → you get **10 Shards (1%)**

It adds up quickly. Your network is your capital.
