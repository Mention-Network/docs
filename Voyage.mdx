**Mention's Voyage** is a social rewards campaign designed to amplify brand discovery in the AI era. Just like Kaito, Voyage empowers you to earn **Shards** by engaging on X (formerly Twitter) with content related to **Mention Network**.

🧭 How to Earn Shards in the Mention Voyage Campaign

### 🔗 1. **Set Up Your Account**

- **Go to the Voyage Dashboard**:
- **Connect your wallet**
- **Connect your X (Twitter) account**

### 🎯 2. **Complete Bonus Quests** _(One-time setup tasks to jumpstart your Shard earnings)_

Earn bonus Shards by completing these simple actions:

- ✅ Follow `@mention_network` on X
- ✅ Join the official **Mention Network Discord**
- ✅ Join the **Telegram community**
- ✅ Check in daily

💡 These are quick ways to boost your early Shard count before diving into main quests.

### 🚀 3. **Complete Voyage Quests** _(Ongoing social engagement = Shards)_

You earn Shards every time you:

- 🐦 Post original tweets or replies that mention **Mention Network** or `@mention_network`
- 💬 Interact (like, reply, quote, retweet) with tweets that mention **Mention Network** or `@mention_network`

🔁 More frequent, high-quality interactions = more Shards\!

### 🏅 4. **Unlock Achievement Badges** _(Big rewards for active participants)_

Each badge gives you bonus Shards on top of your regular earnings:

| Badge                 | Requirement                                                 |
| :-------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🧠 Creator            | Post 50 original tweets mentioning Mention Network          |
| 🔥 Perseverance       | Stay active for 30 consecutive days                         |
| 🌍 Explorer           | Earn 10,000 total Shards                                    |
| 📣 Voice of Community | Accumulate 10,000 likes across your tweets                  |
| 🧵 Raid God           | Interact with 100 tweets by @mention_network                |
| 🤖 AI Companion       | Prompt 1,000 times using your LLMs                          |
| 🧠 Smart Prompts      | Get LLMs to mention 50 different brands                     |
| 🌟 Big Fan            | Get a single brand mentioned 10 times across different LLMs |

🏆 Badges can multiply your influence and boost visibility in the leaderboard or future airdrops.

### 🔄 5. **Shard Distribution Timing**

- Shards are calculated and distributed **every 1 hour**.
- Your rewards update based on your most recent activity and badge progress.
